About

I'm a translational health sciences researcher and biotech consultant based in Windsor, Ontario, with a background in biomedical science, biochemistry, and oncology diagnostics.

I specialize in early-stage biotech — particularly liquid biopsy, multi-cancer early detection, and molecular diagnostics. I founded Setpoint Bio, a Delaware C-Corp developing a ctRNA-based multi-cancer early detection test, which means I've navigated the same funding, regulatory, and commercialization challenges my clients face.

I help biotech founders in the U.S. and Canada write winning NIH SBIR and NCI grants, build regulatory strategy for LDT and FDA pathways, and structure translational research plans that satisfy both scientific reviewers and investors.

If you're pre-revenue or pre-seed and need someone who understands the science as well as the strategy, let's talk.
