About

A scientist and clinician who embeds in your team, without the overhead of a hire.

Samavia is a biomedical scientist with graduate training (BSc + MSc, University of Windsor), years of direct patient care experience, and active experience building EmpiricalDx, a rare disease clinical case atlas. She takes contracts in medical writing, health systems program design, knowledge translation, regulatory affairs, clinical data management, and patient communication, bringing scientific rigor and real clinical context to every engagement.

Engaging Samavia as a contractor means your organization retains full ownership of all work product. She is happy to sign an NDA, and all deliverables belong to you from day one, with none of the costs of benefits, onboarding, or long-term headcount.

Services

What she does

Medical & Scientific Writing

Manuscripts, white papers, regulatory summaries, CME content, patient education materials, and plain language health communications. Translated science that your audience will actually read.

Health Systems & Program Consulting

Program design, needs assessments, resource dissemination strategy, and community feedback integration for health organizations. Helping teams build better initiatives, from idea to implementation.

Knowledge Translation

Turning research evidence into actionable programs, policies, and communications for clinical and community health settings. Bridging the gap between what the evidence says and what organizations actually do.

Regulatory Writing & Strategy

FDA De Novo, 510(k), PCCPs, and Health Canada pathways. SOP development, REB submissions, consent documentation, and regulatory summaries, mapped to your timeline.

Clinical Data & Database Design

REDCap database builds, eCRF design, data dictionaries, study documentation, and clinical data organization for research teams and trial sponsors.

Health Communication Risk Review

Audit of clinical and patient-facing materials for ambiguity, miscommunication risk, and health literacy gaps, before those errors reach patients.

Currently Building

EmpiricalDx

A rare disease clinical case atlas to accelerate MD-PhD collaboration and clinical trials.

EmpiricalDx is being built to compress the distance between scattered clinical signal and the researchers who can act on it. By giving MD-PhDs and trial sponsors a structured, searchable view of rare disease cases, we shorten the path from observation to enrolled cohort.
