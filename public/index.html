About

A consultant with deep technical roots and a builder's instinct for turning science into fundable, defensible programs.

Samavia partners with biotech founders on grant strategy and regulatory pathways for AI-driven diagnostics, devices, and therapeutics — drawing on a translational health sciences background and active experience building EmpiricalDx, a rare disease clinical case atlas.

Regulatory Strategy

Currently Building

EmpiricalDx

A rare disease clinical case atlas to accelerate MD-PhD collaboration and clinical trials.

EmpiricalDx is being built to compress the distance between scattered clinical signal and the researchers who can act on it. By giving MD-PhDs and trial sponsors a structured, searchable view of rare disease cases, we shorten the path from observation to enrolled cohort.
